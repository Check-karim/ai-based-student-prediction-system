About EduPredict AI

Empowering students and educators with machine learning insights

Our Mission

EduPredict AI was built to help students understand their academic trajectory early. By combining study behavior data with a Random Forest machine learning model, we provide early warnings and encouragement before grades slip.

The Technology

Our prediction engine uses scikit-learn's Random Forest classifier trained on academic performance patterns. It evaluates six key factors:

  • Daily study hours
  • Class attendance rate
  • Previous GPA
  • Assignments completed
  • Extracurricular involvement
  • Sleep hours

Performance Categories

At Risk Needs immediate academic intervention
Average On track with room for improvement
High Performer Excellent academic trajectory

Who Can Use It?

Students can register, log in, run predictions, and view their history.

Administrators have a dedicated dashboard to monitor all students and system-wide prediction analytics.
