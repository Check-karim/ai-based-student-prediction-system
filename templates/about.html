About {{ app_name }}

Empowering {{ university_name }} students and administrators with machine learning insights

Our Mission

{{ app_name }} was built for {{ university_name }} to help students understand their academic trajectory early. By combining study behavior data with a Random Forest machine learning model, we provide early warnings and encouragement before grades slip — supporting {{ university_short }}'s goal of producing skilled, employable graduates.

The Technology

Our prediction engine uses scikit-learn's Random Forest classifier trained on academic performance patterns. It evaluates six key factors:

  • Daily study hours
  • Class attendance rate
  • Previous GPA
  • Assignments completed
  • Extracurricular involvement
  • Sleep hours

Performance Categories

At Risk Needs immediate academic intervention
Average On track with room for improvement
High Performer Excellent academic trajectory

Who Can Use It?

Students register with their {{ university_short }} department, run predictions on their dashboard, and view their personal history.

Administrators monitor all registered students and system-wide analytics, and can run the same prediction tool for demos, advising, or model validation.
